The Stars

In this vast universe there are countless stars and planets with different size and color.Stars are the most widely recognized astronomical objects, and represent the most fundamental building blocks of galaxies.

What?

A star is an astronomical object comprising a luminous spheroid of plasma held together by self-gravity.

Where?

The nearest star to Earth is the Sun.

How?

Stars are born within the clouds of dust and scattered throughout most galaxies.
